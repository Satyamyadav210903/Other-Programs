There are various types of books

Romantic

Romantic books, often referred to as romance novels, are a genre of literature that primarily focuses on the romantic relationship between two people. These books typically emphasize the emotional aspects of love and relationships, often incorporating elements of drama, conflict, and passion.

Explore More Books >>
Science fiction

Science fiction, often abbreviated as sci-fi, is a genre of literature that explores imaginative and futuristic concepts grounded in science and technology. These books often delve into speculative ideas about advanced technology, space exploration, time travel, parallel universes, and the impact of scientific advancements on society.

Explore More Books >>
Mysterious

Mysterious books, often classified under the mystery genre, are centered around the solving of a crime or unraveling a puzzle. These books are designed to intrigue and engage readers through suspense, red herrings, and complex plot twists.

Explore More Books >>
Religeous Books

Religious books encompass a broad range of literature that explores, explains, and promotes religious beliefs, practices, and philosophies. These texts can include sacred scriptures, theological works, devotional writings, and books on religious history and practice.

Explore More Books >>
Technical Books

Technical books are specialized publications that provide in-depth information, guidance, and instruction on specific subjects related to science, technology, engineering, and mathematics (STEM), as well as other technical fields like computer science, information technology, and various applied sciences.

Explore More Books >>
Horror Books

Horror books are a genre of literature designed to evoke fear, dread, and suspense in readers. These books often explore themes of the supernatural, psychological terror, and the macabre, creating a sense of unease and anticipation.
